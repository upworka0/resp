Loji by Sandtex

Loji was born out of a need to have logistic software for distribution parts. It has a unique note design which allows for many team members to quickly catch up on orders. It has services that allow for automation - invoice, tracking requests, OA requests, and the like.

The Workflow

  • 1 Upload your orders
  • 2 Start sending out automated emails
  • 4 Begin updating the orders with dates, notes, and statuses
  • 5 Work on orders that are late
  • 6 Complete delivered orders and close them! :)
